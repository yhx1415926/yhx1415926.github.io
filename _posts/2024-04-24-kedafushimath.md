#### Defination:
1.$ R^{n}$
  <p>\begin{align}
  d_{1}(x,y)=\sum_{i=1}^{n}{\left | x_{i}-y_{i} \right | }\\
  d_{2}(x,y)=\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_{i}-y_{i})^{2}}\\
  d_{\infty}(x,y)=\max_{1 \le i \le n}{\left | x_{i}-y_{i} \right | }
  \end{align}</p>
2.$ Q_{p}$
  <p>\begin{align}
  d_{p}(x,y)=|x-y|_{p}=p^{-v_{p}(|x-y|)}
  \end{align}</p>
3.$ A^{n}$
  <p>\begin{align}
  d_{H}(x,y)=\#\{1\le i\le n|x_{i}\ne y_{i}\} 
  \end{align}</p>
